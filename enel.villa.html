    ABOUT ENEL VILLA

    RRETH ENEL VILLA


    ENEL VILLA is a family-run guesthouse offering comfort and hospitalityand stunning views of the Jonian coast.
    Our mission is to make your holiday unforgettable.
    Comfortable Accommodations: Enel Villa in Saranda offers family rooms with sea views, air-conditioning, and private bathrooms.
    Each room includes a kitchenette, dining table, and free toiletries.
    Essential Facilities: Guests enjoy a terrace and balcony, free WiFi, and free on-site private parking.
    Additional amenities include a washing machine, kitchenware, and a TV.
    Convenient Services: The guest house provides private and express check-in and check-out services, luggage storage, and free WiFi.
    Prime Location: Saranda Bay Beach is a 6-minute walk away, offering easy beach access.
    Saranda town center is 1.7km from the property.
    Couples in particular like the location they rated it 8.9 for a two-person trip.
